Cake

Description

This cake is so yummy

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Egg
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix the egg and sugar until you make a soft sponge
  2. Melt the butter and milk to make the cake batter
  3. Slowly pour the butter and milk mixture onto the soft sponge
  4. Add cream to the top
